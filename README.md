This repository serves as the official administrative hub for the Skill Synergy Center, where the chaotic energy of student assignments is refined into the orderly bliss of structured database records through a process we professionally call flipping. By resolving the aggressive SQLSTATE integrity violations that once haunted our primary keys, we have achieved a state of bureaucratic nirvana where every submission is automatically incremented and every foreign key is strictly cascaded into submission. The dashboard now functions as a sterile yet strangely addictive progress tracking station, ensuring that your total XP remains as accurate as a Swiss watch while you pursue your current learning goals within a high-contrast, dark-mode environment that respects your late-night productivity. It is, quite literally, the most exciting way to fill out a digital spreadsheet ever devised by a secondary informatics student.
